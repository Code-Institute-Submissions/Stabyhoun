The
Stabyhoun

Allways by your side!

Origin

The Stabyhoun is a Dutch breed that finds its origin in Friesland. The breed was used a lot as huntingdog, for guarding the yard and to hunt for moles and rats. Since the 60's the breed got more fame outside of Friesland. In the current years the breed is starting to get more and more known outside of the Netherlands. Nowadays the dog is mostly a companion dog inside the house.

The name

In the Netherlands this breed is called Friese Stabij. The first part of the name is from the province of origin, Friesland. The second part of the name is from stand by me. This will also explain the English name "Stabyhoun", Dog that stands by me. Just like the name says, it's a dogbreed that wants to go out and about with the owner!

Sports

Allthough the breed is used a lot as a companion dog, it's definitely a dog that can be used in dog sports. You can train them fairly easily, because it is a breed that likes to work for his boss. The Stabyhoun is a breed that likes to be challenged physically and mentally. Obedience, fetch, hunting, agility, treibball, search and detection. You name it, the Stabyhoun can do it!

Water

Besides sports, this breed also loves to be inside the water. So offcourse you could do some watersports with the Stabyhoun. You can also make this breed happy with just a swim or some playing some fetch around the water. As long as it's something active to do with the owner the Stabyhoun is up for it. At the end of the day the owner gets the challenge of taking the dog home :)
